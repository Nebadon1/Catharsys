About our Group

The Catharsys Group was established to provide efficient, unbiased, expert solutions to governments, businesses and individuals. Our consulting services provide our clients guidance in decision making, offering solutions developed by our experienced analysts. Services include Public Policy Development, Economic Analysis, Rural and Urban Planning, Engineering and Construction, Infrastructure Development, Environmental Assessment and Remediation, Monitoring and Evaluation, and Technology and IT Services. We also offer a host of specialty services to cater to the unique needs of our clients. Our partners and staff serve a wide range of individuals, corporations, partnerships, and non-profit organizations and are experts in the areas and issues that impact our clients. Our consulting services offer you the opportunity to develop strategies insuring your organization's success. Our unbiased approach and rigorous methods are designed to provide tailored solutions to meet your specific needs. Additionally, The Catharsys Group has several staff members directly involved in hurricane response and reconstruction efforts. We are presently working the Caribbean providing much needed services, building capacity, and assisting in adaptation planning
